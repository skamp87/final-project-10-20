About Duden's Designs

Hi! I'm Susan, your friendly neighborhood seamstress.
I am also the owner and operator of Duden's Designs.

I started sewing in 2016 to accommodate my youngest and his cloth diapers.
Nothing fit! It was so frustrating until I found maxaloones.
So I took a dive into more patterns, then fabrics, then custom fabrics.
Now here I am!
If there's a pattern for it, I can make it.
